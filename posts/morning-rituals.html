Morning Rituals

Every morning, I make the same cup of tea. The ritual is simple: boil water, steep leaves, wait. But in this repetition, I find comfort and a moment to gather my thoughts before the day begins.

There's something beautiful about the consistency of small rituals. They ground us in a world that often feels chaotic and unpredictable.
